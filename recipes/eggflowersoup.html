Egg Flower Soup

Traditional Chinese egg flower soup

(with a secret ingredient that makes it divine.)

Ingredients

  • 2 cups chicken broth
  • White pepper to taste
  • 2 dashes sesame oil
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons peas
  • 1 green onion, chopped

Directions

  1. Bring the chicken broth, white pepper, and sesame oil to a boil in a saucepan.
  2. While stirring slowly in one direction to get the soup moving, pour the beaten egg into the soup in a slow but steady stream.
  3. Gently stir in the peas.
  4. Ladle into serving bowls; sprinkle with green onion to serve.
